# Fancy Treasure - Wilds Unknown Set Championship

Saturday morning - the best time for a set championship at the Fancy Treasure in Biblis. I was late for sign up as originally some other family events were scheduled for the day. With me there were 18 players participating to win the foil Woody - a chase card as it's a good artwork and as well playable. Details can be seen on https://tcg.ravensburgerplay.com/events/604377. With 18 players it meant we had to play five rounds of Swiss and then a top cut starting with top 8.
