### Round 1

Round 1 pairing was with Oliver and his 🟢🔵 Emerald Sapphire aggressive deck which is build around gaining a lot of lore in a single turn. Oliver especially utilized the support mechanism together with the new Gadget and the old Alice. There is quite a good synergy in this - but it banks on having the characters stay on board. In the first game I could exert the key characters and remove them in time as well as utilize the Cheshire Cat. In the second game Oliver could get this one big lore turn in to win the game before I could play my exert effect. In the last game I could bring the exert effects into play a lot and by this control the board from the early on stages.

### Round 2

Round 2 pairing was with Jan and his 🟡🟢 Amber Emerald dogs variant fielding the new Mickey combined with Snow Fort and Under the Sea. In the first game Jan made a mistake from the Mickey effects choosing the wrong characters of mine for the strength reduction, so I could trade then back utilizing the Mother Gothel to offset the effect from the Snow Fort. Second game was quickly scooped by Jan as he drew into too many uninkables to even play a proper turn two.
