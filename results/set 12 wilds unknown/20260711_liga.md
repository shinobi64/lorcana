### Round 1

Round 1 pairing was with Leo and his 🟡🔴 Amber Ruby boosties deck. This deck does not play as many characters as it aims to establish the boost characters and the use the Webby's Diary to draw cards of the boost action. I could establish a bit of a wider board state initially but Leo could discard my Chromicon early on. I was then lucky to find two Lilo which I could recurr and then create pressure with them. In the second game I just went wide and then turned into Elinor to win the game.
