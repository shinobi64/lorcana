# Disney Lorcana Challenge Event Dortmund 2026

Third main lorcana challenge in the second competetive season. Finally again in Germany the Westfalenhalle in Dortmund was the location for this event - again an event location for exhibitions and fairs - repurposed with two halls for the main event and some additional activities. Like in Ghent I decided to play on Friday and was accompanied by Anni and the kids.

The tournament was played over a total of 12 rounds of swiss followed by a top cut to 32 players. The first 8 rounds of swiss were played on the first day and all players achieving at least 18 points in the best of three format would advance to day two for swiss. The event was completely run via the Ravensburger Playhub https://tcg.ravensburgerplay.com/events/648791.

As the tournament was run in the infinity format contrary to the otherwise used core constructed format it was harder to prepare - and most of it happened using the online platforms. The meta for infinity was fast developing and constantly changing. Main decks to consider have been 🟡🟢 Amber Emerald Aggro with Dogs or with Snow Fort, 🟡⚪️ Amber Steel as songs or as aggressive version, 🔴🔵 Ruby Sapphire in items version or in shark version. After this some lower level decks also had good win rates.

## Tournament Rounds

After all the testing I decided to go with an Ruby Sapphire Shark build as I still enjoy the playstyle and found it very good into the slower decks while having a chance into the more aggressive decks thanks to the Be Prepared and the Olaf cards. Still knowing that the aggressive decks would be a tough call.
