# Card Club - Wilds Unknown PreRelease

PreRelease event for the new set 12 Wilds Unknown in the Card Club. As always it happened on the evening before the pre release date. 18 players showed up with high interest in the new cards. Detailed tracking via https://tcg.ravensburgerplay.com/events/511499

## Card Club Weekly Liga Meta Report

### Spieler 18

All on sealed decks

## Tournament Rounds

### Round 1

First round was with Yasin - a player that came over again to Lorcana from Altered. In the first game he had the great combination with Three Arrows and some additional trades to have my complete board removed. I could play the same on my side in game two and in the last game we were in a stale mate setup - however he then drew the better endgame cards to take over the end game.
