# Mochi Cards - Wilds Unknown Set Championship

Mochi Cards holds the set championship typically on a Saturday - the same was in the current set. Originally it was planned that Anni and the kids would attend but they had some other topics to take care for - so I attended alone. Details can be seen on https://tcg.ravensburgerplay.com/events/558566. With 14 players it meant we had to play four rounds of Swiss and then a top cut starting with top 4.

### Round 2

Round 2 pairing was with Florian from the 7 Serpents team. He was on the 🟡🟢 Amber Emerald toys deck. This deck can go quite aggressive - despite not as aggressive as the dogs deck but could establish more card draw with Woody. In both games the Olaf helped me to claw the games back and then the control cards like Shark helped to control the board. As well the Elisa Maza on board prevented the main removal as the strength of my characters could not be reduced.

### Top 4

With the 3-0-1 I managed to get into the top 4 at position one. This paired me then into Matthias with his 🟡🔴 Amber Ruby Midrange deck. In the first game he was able to discard my Diary early on and I could not find one for a long time, while he could establish it earlier and then have card advantage. Second game was then normal game play on my side with the Diary in play. In the last game Matthias surprised me with two Mowgli played after each other to discard my Diary again and then establish one by himself. Again this put me on the back foot from which I could not recover in timely manner.
