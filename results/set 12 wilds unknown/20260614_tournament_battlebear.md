Details can be seen on https://tcg.ravensburgerplay.com/events/604225. With 19 players it meant we had to play five rounds of Swiss and then a top cut starting with top 8.

### Top 8

With the 3-2-0 I managed to get into the top 8 at position six. This paired me then into Andre with his 🟡⚪️ Amber Steel Songs deck. His deck has some special cards like Mob Song and Tiana to counter the meta. In the first game Andre got very wide - but I could take the Under the Sea from the top of the deck to remove the whole board - and Andre only had the Mob Song left in hand. In the second game I kept two Ursula to snipe the songs from his hand and he was then a bit ink screwed and could not find any additional song cards.
