Details can be seen on https://tcg.ravensburgerplay.com/events/586175. With 13 players it meant we had to play four rounds of Swiss and then a top cut starting with top 4.

### Round 2

Round 2 pairing was with Matthias and his kind of signature 🟡🔴 Amber Ruby boost deck. In the first game he could establish the double Diary and then find regularly boost characters to give him the needed card draw. As well he could establish the Cauldron, so I ran out of options to deal with this. In the second game I could go faster and then remove his draw options and then quest out. In the last game he drew in the mid game only non boost cards which made his hand size pretty small and let him less options to go.

### Round 3

Round 3 was a pairing with Philipp and his 🟡🟢 Amber Emerald dogs based deck. Despite my deck having options it's still a very tough matchup. In the first game I could not bring down a meaningful two drop, so with only two cards with one willpower on board it was easy for Philipp to take advantage of the Elinor line and then swing tempo even more into his favor. Second game was different as I could establish a better board and then Philipp got stuck with two Under the Sea in hand. In the last game I got lucky to top deck a Hades which I could immediately play and then refill my hand ad contest the board for the rest of the game.

### Top 4

With the 3-0-1 I again managed to have the top place after the swiss rounds. However as place four was Johannes with this 🔵⚪️ Sapphire Steel deck I knew a tough matchup was ahead of me. In the first game I could use my play advantage and play a good curve and remove his cards quickly, especially the Goliath. In the second game Johannes had the magic line and the game was over after he could remove my Hercules with Let it Go and then play a Goliath down. The last game was very back and forth - however Johannes had two very strong draws after each other to remove again double characters on my end and I had not enough challenging power back on board to remove all threats at the same time then any more.
