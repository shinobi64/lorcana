### Round 1

Round 1 pairing was with Anni and his 🟣⚪️ Amethyst Steel aggressive deck which is build around Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs allowing Dwarfs to come out early and gain card draw effects from it. In the first game we had a real grind where she could bring out the Dwarfes early and then always contest my board by playing Demonas. We played for over 35 minutes in this game one. Second game I was a bit quicker and had my exert effects in play early on and then could finish in overtime using the Maximus triggers.

### Round 3

In the third round Christian and his 🟣⚪️ Amethyst Steel aggressive deck. It's similar to be build from Annika last Thursday but included even more burn cards like the new Dash Parr. In the first game Christian had the good curve with Strength on turn four to basically remove my board and quest of from there. In the second game I could use my turn advantage to exert the Cheshire Cat directly and by this take the tempo onto my side. Last game Christian missed the one drop and due to this his curve did not work out for singing Strength - on the flip side I could then exert again and take over the game tempo.

### Round 4

Last round pairing was with Alexander and his 🟡🟣 Amber Amethyst control deck. This deck can go fast but as well have a lot of control options with Dale, Demona and Raging Storm included. In both games I could play out the exert effects to my advantage on removing the high value characters and use the Mother Gothel for better challenges. Lastly in both games the Maximus closed down the last missing lore points.
