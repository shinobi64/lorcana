Round 1 pairing was with Oliver and his 🟢🔵 Emerald Sapphire aggressive deck which is build around gaining a lot of lore in a single turn. As I have played against this deck last week already I knew how to play against this. My deck utilized the exert effects and the damage moving to the best possible extend. Based on this I could win both games rather easy.

### Round 2

Round 2 pairing was with Annika and her 🟣⚪️ Amethyst Steel aggressive deck. This deck tries to utilize the synergy between Pocahontas and Christoper Robin to gain lore fast. In the first game I could deploy my Mother Gothel early on and then use the exerts as good as possible and dominate with my evasives. In the second game Annika could go very fast and I had not much to counter the very wide board. Last game was a real grind as we both did not had much to play early on - then however I was able to play Isis to exert a Lexington and due to the number of cards in hand he remained exerted and was then a viable target for my shift Mulan - which cleared the board. After this Annika was able to play multiple Demonas which challenged me - but I was able to wrap it with the better characters on board.
