### Round 2

Second round was with the other Andre and his deck - he had an insane pool of removals, very good top end cards and a lot of evasive characters. I could win game one by playing to the board and finding my good characters. In the second and third game Andre won as he drew into his evasive cards and I could not counter them quickly enough to prevent the lore gain.
