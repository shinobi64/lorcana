# Wizards Well - Wilds Unknown Set Championship

Sunday - the typical time for a set championship at the Wizards Well in Mannheim. This time the event took place during the heat wave period in Germany so it was a hot event both in sense of card play and physical conditions. Details can be seen on https://tcg.ravensburgerplay.com/events/506148. With 19 players it meant we had to play five rounds of Swiss and then a top cut starting with top 8.

In the fist game I could run with my game plan and outgrind the game finally with the Olafs. ### Top 4

Top 4 pairing was then with Philipp from our team with his 🟡🟢 Amber Emerald dogs deck. I was on the play but could not capitalize from it as he found a very good line in both games. In the first with the double Elionor and double Willow line, and in the second one with the two Lilos off the first Tramp wheel. Both moves made the games way to fast for my deck to respond and gain control back. Especially the Elionors allowed Philipp to keep a healthy hand size and I could not benefit from my additional card draw.
