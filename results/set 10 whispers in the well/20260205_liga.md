## Casual Rounds

In the casual rounds before the actual event I played a decklist with some next set cards already proxied in. It was fun to play again with a rather decent Ruby Sapphire list that has the potential to cope with the meta decks

### Match 1

First match was with Matthias and his aggressive Amethyst Emerald burn list. In all three games I was able to cope with it - until he pulled out nearly all Gathering Knowledge and Wisdom cards at the same time. Still in the games I lost I was one turn short on my side to win the game - which is much closer than I had it with other Sapphire decks.

### Round 1

First round pairing was with Felix who was on Amethyst Steel instead of Emerald Sapphire to my surprise. However on the flip side he was surprised by my deck choice too. I could bring on the early aggression as well as the follow up with Tiana and Pluto to overwhelm the possible reactions by Felix. It worked in both games, while in the second game Felix tried to race and I had to break his win condition on board to force him to have another Cobra - which he did not had.
