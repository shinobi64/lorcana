### Round 1

Round 1 pairing was with Gabriel and his Amber Sapphire control deck. This is a suitable matchup for me, as my top end cards are a bit better and by his removal options he ramps me up quicker into these higher cards. In both games the location was really good, as he had no real outs against it. So it could tick away for lore and collect cards for later use.

### Round 2

Second round was with Alex and his Amber Steel deck. In the first game he had the line into early Angel and then Goliath to break down my characters and quest away. In the second game I got better onto the board and could establish some characters there to then take the win in the end. In the last game Alex drew very very poorly with two times paired Docs, so he could only draw once out of them. I on the flip side had a shift Mulan with Medallion Weights on board - basically preventing his side to quest.

Last round was with Philipp and his Amber Amethyst deck - this is a very grindy list which intends to stall the opponent as much as possible to then slam libraries onto the field and protect them at all means. In the first game I could race until a certain point but made a playing mistake with not taking back cards from my location before it was banished. In the second game he just had it.
