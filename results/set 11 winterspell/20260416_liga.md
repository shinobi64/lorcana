### Round 1

Round 1 pairing was with Gabriel. This time he changed from Amber Sapphire to Ruby Amethyst - which surprised me a bit. I lost the dice roll and in the first game Gabriel could deploy an early Library against which I had no challenging power. In the second and third game I could bring the Rhino early to the game and then scale the board from there. This helped to go wide and then accelerate the tempo.

### Round 3

Last round was with Philipp - kind of usual the end game. Philipp was on Amethyst Sapphire this week which is a rough matchup for Amber Steel. In the first game I could play quickly, remove his early game and he only found actions but no bodies to play. In the second game I could start well but he played two Cheshire Cats followed by Demona which then kind of wiped my board. In the last game as well the Cheshire Cat carried his game as I could not remove it and only had Lilos to play - which is bad given a Cat on the other side.
