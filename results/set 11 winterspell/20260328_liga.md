### Round 4

Last round was with Matthias and his Amber Ruby build. This was using the Raging Storm and the Scrooge Countinghouse, similar like I played it on the last liga event in Hockenheim. The deck has a lot of control and wins always the long game, so my chance was only to race. I could manage this in the first game, while in the second one he was to quick on the board with the boost characters. In the last game I raced but could not close due to timeout and end of extra turns - so we closed this as a draw.
