### Round 1

First round was Philipp and his Ruby Amethyst deck. In the first game I could establish the needed tempo to run through with the lore gain. In the second game Philipp could bounce back the Scrooge multiple times for the card advantage and clear my evasives with the Headless Horseman. In the last game I could not find evasives to answer the opposing Genie. Donald was good in the second game, as it forced Philipp to have the Scrooge boosted to get him off the board.

Last round was against Christian and his Ruby Amethyst deck. In the first game I was stuck on the four ink turn with only drawing into uninkables which hurt me from there on in every turn. In the second game I made a crucial mistake not taking out his Cheshire Cat and by this clearing the full board. This costed me the game together with not finding answers to his evasive characters.
