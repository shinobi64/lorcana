# Disney Lorcana Challengen Qualifier Leipzig 2026

Second CCQ for me, after the last one in Stuttgart a second time in Leipzig during the CAGGTUS LAN event. It's a typical exebition environnebt - however they had a designated area for TCG and Tabletop gaming this year. Tournament was originally scheduled for 512 players but based on registrations it was cut back to 256 players - this meant 8 rounds of swiss on Saturday followed by a cut to top32 which would then play on Sunday.The event was completely run via the Ravensburger Playhub https://tcg.ravensburgerplay.com/events/493342

### Round 7

In round 7 I was paired with Aris out of the Munich area. He played a 🟢🔵 Emerald Sapphire Clarabelle deck - which is an even to slightly unfavored matchup for me. In the first game he had the Prince Philipp combo with Malicious, Mean and Scary twice as well twice the shift Clarabelle to keep his hand healty. In the second game I missed my ramp but played double one drop on turn two to get for a quick tempo early on - this created too much lore pressure in the early game which he could not answer. The last game was a real grind - I could get a good start with Tigger followed by Elsa to clear his early characters but then a continious back and forth. I kept questing and he missed out on getting his hand size healthy and was forced to play an Inkgeyser - which I could luckily answer by a combination from Sail the Azurite Sea and Tipo to bring me back up to six ink even faster than he was able to ramp back up. This allowed me to bring out my bigger characters again and quest to the 20 lore.
