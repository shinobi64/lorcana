### Round 1

Round 1 pairing was with Paul and his Emerald Sapphire aggro deck. This deck contains a lot of two lore quest characters and some actions to re-ready characters and then quest again. I could use in the first and third game the Ralph banish ability to clear multiple characters on Paul's side. In the second game I had not such a removal and by this Paul could quest through with his board.

### Round 2

Second round was with Philipp and his Amethyst Sapphire deck. His list was more or less stock with the exception of two Wasabi characters. In the first game I could apply some pressure but then top decked no characters to close the game. Additionally I could not remove the Dumbo - which then made it possible for Philipp to gain resource advantage. In the second game it was more or less the same, I stabilized the game and then the Dumbo made Dumbo things and drew cards - which finally allowed Philipp to close the game.

### Round 3

Last round was with Oliver and his Amethyst Steel deck. In the first game we were both close and then Oliver top decked the White Rose to gain the last missing lore. In the second game I bricked on four ink and due to this could not play the Horseman to clear Olivers board. This then hit me back as he played in the next turn Angel to clear out my board and then I had no chance to rebuild anymore.
