### Round 1

Round 1 pairing was with Annika and her 🟡🔴 Amber Ruby midrange deck. This deck can only be beaten by fast and aggressive questing as otherwise it would draw endless cards and gain value via the Cauldron too. I could in both games my Lilos setup early, as they help after the Raging Storm to still have value on board. In both games Annika struggled with inkable cards, so I could quest to victory.

### Round 2

Round 2 pairing was with Markus and his 🟣⚪️ Amethyst Steel deck. He won the dice roll and had the perfect start line in game one - I could close the gap mid game and have a deciding board in play, but he top decked the needed Cobra for the final lore gain. In the second game I could get my board established early on and control it to gain the needed lore advantage. In the last game Markus started again with all the needed cards in hand, especially removing my Rhino shift line - we then battled card for card, however he drew into the Doc and found two Strength of the Raging Fire from it - effectively ending the game there.
