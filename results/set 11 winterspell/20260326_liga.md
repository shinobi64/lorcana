### Round 1

Round 1 pairing was with Gabriel and he had an Amber Sapphire control deck in play. In the first game he missed out on the ramp and by this I could establish my boost characters and draw cards. In the second game he could find the perfect line into multiple ramp steps combined with card draw from the Surfer Stitch. In the last game I could pressure again early with the locations.

### Round 3

Last round was with Philipp and his Amethyst Sapphire Blurple deck. Unfortunately he knew which deck I played and as he has played the deck himself, he knew how to play around the board wipe and use his card draw efficiently. He had in both games multiple Hades on his hand to answer my Half Sharks played. It remains that you basically need the 2 drop Scrooge to effectively start into the match and get the card draw going.
