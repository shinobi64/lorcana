### Round 1

First round was a bye due to the fact that we have been an uneven number of players.

Result after round 1: **1-0-0**

### Round 2

Second round was with Philipp in a classic mirror match, as he was playing on the Amethyst Sapphire deck too. I won the dice roll and could play down in the first game pretty quickly and gain lore and by this reach the 20 first. In the second game I was bricked with three out of three Genie around turn four - subsequently I lost the game in the longer run, as I could not accelerate early on enough. In the last game I missed the mid/ late game good top decks - with only having Tipo and Royal Guard, while Philipp top decked one Hades after the other.
